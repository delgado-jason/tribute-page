Steve Jobs

A Pioneer of the personal computer revolution

"The Only Way To Do Great Work Is To Love What You Do."

Life Timeline

  • 1955 Born February 24th
  • 1972 Attended Reed College
  • 1974 Travelled through India seeking enlightenment
  • 1976 He an Wozniak co-founded Apple
  • 1977 Gained fame and fortune with the production of Apple 2
  • 1983 Developed the Apple Lisa
  • 1984 Macintosh was born. The first mass-produced computer with a GUI
  • 1985 Forced out of Apple by the CEO John Sulley
  • 1986 Co-founded Pixar with its debut movie, Toy Story
  • 1997 Returned to Apple as its new CEO
  • 2001 The original Mac OS was replaced with the new Mac OSX
  • 2003 was diagnosed with a pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor
  • October 5th, 2011 Died of respiratory arrest due to the tumor. He was 56
